Hofje

Historic farmhouse Hofje

Escape to the peaceful village of Martínkovice where this charming holiday home awaits, offering the ideal retreat for groups of up to 12 guests. Surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, it provides a serene sanctuary far from the stresses of everyday life.
The spacious interior features 4 comfortable bedrooms, each having it's own bathroom, a fully equipped kitchen, and a cozy living area ideal for socializing. Step outside to discover a refreshing outdoor swimming pool and a large garden—an ideal setting for barbecues and outdoor fun. Your stay is made even more enjoyable with English, Dutch, and German-speaking hosts, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.
