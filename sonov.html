Historic country house Haus Frilo

Located in the charming village of Šonov, this delightful holiday home comfortably accommodates up to 6 guests. The cozy interior includes 3 well-appointed bedrooms, a modern kitchen, 1 bathroom and a welcoming living space perfect for relaxing after a day of exploration. Outside, a lovely garden provides a serene spot for outdoor dining and leisure activities. With its peaceful surroundings and close proximity to nature trails to hike & bike, this Šonov holiday home is an ideal destination for a relaxing and enjoyable stay. If you would like to take a bike ride and do not have it with you, we will be happy to lend it to you. We can offer you trout from our own fishing ponds after you have ordered them from us in advance. Your stay is made even more enjoyable with English, Dutch, and German-speaking hosts, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.
